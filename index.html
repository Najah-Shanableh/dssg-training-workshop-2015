General Information

The goal of the DSSG Bootcamp is to help data scientist fellows get more done in less time and with less pain by teaching them basic lab skills for data mangement, analysis, and visualization. This hands-on workshop will cover basic concepts and tools, including program design, version control, data management, data analysis, and task automation. Participants are encouraged to help one another throughout the training and to apply what they have learned to their summer projects. Much of the material in this workshop we have modified from the fantastic course materials developed bySoftware Carpentry, Data Carpentry and the Coalition for Open Data Education.
